    Ode to Unending Love

    Ooh! How nice is love between two new lover buds,
    From the continuous butterflies to unending talks at night,
    The talks that resolve no conflict, yet reflect the light
    Of a love so pure, so new, it has only just begun
    Two hearts intertwined, beating as one.

    Ooh my love, how and why did you dare?
    To fall in love with me before consulting your Maker there.
    Are you sure you shall unlove me from this very point?
    When your heart already loved me, and mine loved you in joint?
    Oh, I pity the thought of parting ways,
    For you shall never forget these precious days.

    What love you entered with me, unmountable in might,
    A love you risked for me, radiant and bright.
    I promise, my dear, you wont get over this,
    The love I once gave is a gift, a bliss.
    Weve built our future; its already begun,
    Together, forever, under the sun.

    Just come back, and let our love reignite,
    As it was before, in its glorious light.
    For you shall suffer to find the love I gave
    A love so unyielding, so tender, so brave.
    I tell you, my love, yes, baby, I mean it,
    You wont find this love anywhere, not even a bit.

    Oh, the miscommunication, Ooh my lover, lets resolve,
    Before it bursts its streams and dissolves.
    For our love is unmountable and unseparable,
    Two individuals in love, strong and unbreakable.

    Ooh my love, why did you decide to ghost me,
    And leave me adrift in this loves vast sea?
    I was there to listen, to love, to care,
    Yet you vanished, leaving my heart bare.
    I called, I texted, no reply came through,
    I searched for your voice, your love, your hue.

    Ooh my love, up to when shall I roam?
    My love is dying, longing for its home.
    It was not when I decided to search you out,
    That I found you, by chance, without a doubt.
    There you were, on the streets that day,
    But my words had fled; I had nothing to say.

    Frustration consumed me; I felt so lost,
    I had given my all, regardless of cost.
    Oh, my love, how I long for the moment to speak,
    For the love I hold within makes me weak.
    I tossed a promise, “We shall talk,” I said,
    Yet the silence lingered, and hope seemed dead.

    Still, my love, to this day, I stand,
    With my heart in yours, my outstretched hand.
    You shall remain, no matter what,
    A love to my heart that cannot be forgot.
    Ooh my love, I am here to stay,
    To tell you, I am yours, forever and a day.

    Let us reignite the fire, rebuild what was lost,
    For true loves worth is beyond any cost.
    Together, lets soar, like birds in the sky,
    For our love is eternalit shall never die.
