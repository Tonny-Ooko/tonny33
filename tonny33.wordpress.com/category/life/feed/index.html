A turtle confined in its shell, so tight,
Yet pushes ahead through storm and night.

Its armor may crack, but still, it tries,
Like hearts that heal as dawn will rise.

Through trials we fall, but stand again,
For strength is forged in loss and pain.

One step, one breath, one day at a time,
The mountain is climbed, the stars will align.

No need to fret, no need for fear,
For hope still whispers, “Persevere.”
